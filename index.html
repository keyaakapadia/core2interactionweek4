Ulvales

Gutweed (Ulva intestinalis) at low tide in Brofjorden, Govik, Lysekil Municipality, Sweden. The tublar fronds are puffed up by oxygen from photosynthesis. This specimen is about 7 cm (2.8 in) high and it grows on a rusty boat ramp. In this region, the tidal range is about 20 cm (7.9 in).
